How we met

We got to know each other in Portugal, when Estefy was living and studying
Portuguese in Lisbon. We met for the first time in a party in February 2012.

We have been together ever since, getting to know better one another and enjoying
the Lisbon’s life, knowing however that their joint stay in Portugal had a deadline.
In October 2012 we had to go in different directions, Estefy going back to Mexico
and Hugo leaving one day after to Berlin. It was not a “goodbye” nevertheless,
it was more like a “see you later”, the first of many.

We have never lost contact and after 9 months of distance and missing one another,
we got together again, when Estefy came to Europe in a business trip. It was a very emotional
reencounter, especially not knowing if, and when we would see each other again
we tried to enjoy to the maximum our moments together, before Estefy’s return to Mexico.

Not holding the distance anymore, Hugo went to visit Estefy and get to know
Mexico for the first time in February 2014. In this reencounter Hugo fell in love with Mexico
and both fell even more in love, deciding that they would not live apart anymore
and planning a life together.

In August 2014 Estefy joins Hugo in Berlin to, at last and without anymore farewells,
be happy and enjoy life together!

After some time together and some setbacks, we have decided to take the next step and get married,
in a reserved and civil way in Lisbon on the 31st of December. Our intention was always
to be able to celebrate our wedding with you all, and as so, we are doing it in a religious way
in 2017, on the 29th April in Mexico City, where we are counting on your presence
to make this day even more special!
