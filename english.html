How we met

Estefy and Hugo got to know each other in February 2012, when she was
living and studying Portuguese in Lisbon. We met for the first time in a party,
and have been together ever since. We took every chance we had to get to know
each other better and enjoy our time in Lisbon, knowing fully well
that our time together in Portugal was limited.

In October 2012 we had to go our seperate ways. Estefy went back to Mexico
and Hugo left the following day to Berlin. This was not a “goodbye”, it was
rather more like a “see you later”. The first of many.

We never lost contact and after 9 months of countless emails, phone calls, sms,
we got to meet each other again when Estefy came to Europe on a business trip.
It was a very emotional reencounter, especially not knowing if, and when
we would see each other again. We decided to enjoy every moment we had together,
to the fullest before Estefy’s return to Mexico.

Unable to bear the distance between them anymore, Hugo went to visit Estefy in
February 2014 and got to know Mexico for the first time. Hugo fell in love with the country
and even more in love with Estefy. It was at this point that they realised that
they could not live apart anymore and decided to plan a life together.

In August 2014 Estefy joins Hugo in Berlin, this time, no more farewells.
This was the beginning of a happy and enjoyful life together!

Through our time together, our ups and lows, overcoming obstacles that came our way,
which brought us even closer and stronger, we have decided to take the next step.
On the 31st of December we had a private Civil wedding. Our intention was always
to be able to celebrate our wedding also with friends and family, this is why on the
29th of April 2017, we will be having our church wedding in Mexico City
and would be happy if you could grace the occasion with your presence.
